The sea is full of life.
Fishes are the most famous inhabitants of the sea. There are many of them, differing in size, color, and shape.
Crustacean like lobsters live on the sea floor. They like to dwell next to coral formations and seaweed.
Coral reefs are pretty common on the sea floor. They offer shelter to smaller animals from predators.
Dolphins are probably the most intelligent and funny animals living in the sea. They are really playful creatures.
When I'm out at sea, so far away from the coast that I can't even see the city lights, that's when I feel really free. The sound of the waves hitting the hull of my boat gives me a sense of peace. I look at the flat sea, nothing is in front of me for many miles, but I know that just below the surface life is everywhere.
-Unknown fisherman
